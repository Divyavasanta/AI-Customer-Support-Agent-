# Refund Policy Rules
1. Refunds only allowed within 30 days of purchase. Over 30 days = Reject.
2. Order status MUST be "delivered". If "pending" or "shipped", reject it.
3. Clothing: Full refund if within 30 days.
4. Electronics: Valid within 30 days, but deduct a 10% restocking fee.
5. Digital Goods: No refunds ever.
6. EXCEPTION: If status is "damaged_in_transit", give a full refund immediately (ignore 30-day rule and categories).
